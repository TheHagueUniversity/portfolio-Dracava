Bespoke Design

In my current course User Experience Design I got the assignment of creating a digital gift giving experience. We were supposed to research a user and make our entire design bespoke for this person. We had two phases: one included research and the other one was focused on designing. On the left you can see my user portait from the research phase.

Our end product consisted of three differen parts:

  • Card Personalisation tool
  • Personalised card
  • Digital Unwrapping

    • Hallmark

    The main goal was designing an experience that would also be useful for our client, Hallmark. They are a card designing company and they wanted new ideas to improve their business.
